Dorm Room

A charcoal drawing of my dorm room from freshman year. A study on fabric and how it interacts with objects around it as well as highlights and shadows. I included multiple different fabrics from pillows, to blankets, to stuffed animals.

Confession

A two point perspective drawing on the confession booth inside the Saint Joseph Chapel at Seton Hill. Using a two point perspective, I was able to make the booth look three dimensional and well proportioned compared to the rest of the room.

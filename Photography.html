Have a Safe Day

This is a photograph taken at the Connellsville Railroads. A worker would use this bridge to get down to the tracks, featuring a sign telling them to Have a Safe Day. This picture is meant to encapsulate the history of Connellsville and its railroads, marking its time in the coke production industry.

Shot on a Canon Rebel T7

Lacrosse

Taken while doing a work study with the men's lacrosse team. Balancing aperture, shutter speed and ISO to capture high impact moments during the game. These photos would be used to promote their team on social media, which has seen an increase in followers and engagement.

Shot on a Canon EOS R7

Can't Sleep

A lot of people can relate to struggling to sleep some nights. Lying awake on your phone or just staring at the ceiling. I create this image to capture the dread and out of body experience this can sometimes create. Using a long exposure camera, I laid as still as I could but not enough to keep me from being blured.

Shot on a Canon Rebel T7

Christmas Carol Book Cover

A book cover for The Christmas Carol by Charles Dickens. The illustration was done in ibisPaint and brought together in Illustrator. This book cover uses imagery of the ghosts of Christmas past, present and future to hint the reader into the mysterious story.

Created in Adobe Illustrator and ibisPaint

Lacrosse 2026 Schedule Poster

A poster to showcase the 2026 season of the Seton Hill Men's Lacrosse team to promote their upcoming games. Posted around the school and given to parents, this poster serves as an easy visual for people to look at and know what games are upcoming and whether they are home or away.

Created in Adobe Illustrator

Feel the Beat

A poster created for the movie Feel the Beat starring Sofia Carson. It's a story about a dancer who struggles to make her own on the stage, decides to help out a local dance studio to pass the love of dance onto the younger dancers who look up to her.

Created in Adobe Illustrator

Hi, I’m Bailey Traynor from Connellsville PA, and I am a graphic designer and photographer who focuses on historical photography; capturing those blink and you’ll miss moments that people want to remember. Memories have been a huge influence on my art, from wanting to remember the past while helping people to create memories in the present, so that people can look back on them in the future. I’ve worked with local historical organizations to photograph the renovations of buildings and worked with sports teams to watch people grow and things change with time.

I work in both physical and digital media, from pencil, paint, and plaster to drawing tablets and computers. I’m experienced in a wide variety of Adobe programs such as Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, and Lightroom. I’m always willing to learn new things and try new programs to expand my knowledge.
